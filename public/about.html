About VitalSoftAI

We build the software layer under health research.

Founded by engineers and researchers who spent too many nights reconciling spreadsheets before a submission deadline, VitalSoftAI exists to make the infrastructure behind health research as rigorous as the research itself.

Why we exist

Research is slowed more by data plumbing than by science.

Every research team we've worked with has the same story: strong hypotheses, capable people, and a data pipeline held together with exports, emails, and trust. We think that's a solvable engineering problem, not a permanent cost of doing research — so we build the platform that removes it.

How we work

Embedded with your team, not handed off to it.

We build alongside research operations, biostatistics, and IT from day one, so the systems we ship match how your team actually works — and stay maintainable long after launch.

How we got here

A short, deliberate path.

2020

Started inside a hospital research unit

Built as an internal tool to unify trial data across three clinics before becoming its own company.

2022

First platform customers

Expanded beyond the founding hospital network to academic and biotech research partners across Canada.

2024

Introduced research-grade AI tooling

Added cohort discovery and predictive modelling, built and validated alongside biostatistics teams.

Today

A full platform for the research data lifecycle

From capture to analysis to compliance — supporting 40+ research and hospital networks.

What guides the work

Three things we don't compromise on.

Data integrity

Every transformation is traceable back to its source. If we can't explain where a number came from, it doesn't ship.

Privacy by default

Access controls and consent tracking aren't a module — they're built into how every system handles data from the start.

Built to be understood

Your team should be able to reason about the system we leave behind, not just operate it.
