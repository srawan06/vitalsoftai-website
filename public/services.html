Services

Every stage of the
research data lifecycle.

We build with your research and IT teams rather than handing over a black box — each service below can stand alone or connect into a single platform.

FIG. 01

Clinical Data Platform

A single system for capturing, validating, and versioning study data across sites — replacing spreadsheet hand-offs with one auditable pipeline from case-report form to analysis-ready dataset.

  • Multi-site data capture with real-time validation rules
  • Full data lineage and version history for audits
  • Role-based access down to the field level
FIG. 02

Research Analytics & AI

Models trained on your data and protocols — not a generic layer bolted on top — for cohort discovery, outcome prediction, and literature review at a pace that keeps up with your study timelines.

  • Cohort discovery and stratification tooling
  • Predictive models reviewed alongside your biostatisticians
  • AI-assisted literature synthesis with source traceability
FIG. 03

EHR & Systems Integration

Standards-based connections into hospital and lab information systems keep research datasets current automatically, cutting the manual re-entry that slows most multi-site studies down.

  • HL7 FHIR-based interfaces to EHR/EMR systems
  • Lab and imaging system connectors
  • Scheduled or event-driven data sync
FIG. 04

Compliance & Governance

Consent management, granular access control, and full audit trails, built to PIPEDA and PHIPA standards from the start — not added on before a review.

  • Participant consent tracking, end to end
  • Field-level access control and de-identification
  • Exportable audit logs for ethics and regulatory review
